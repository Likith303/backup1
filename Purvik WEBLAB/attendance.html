ATTENDANCE SYSTEM

B.M.S. College of Engineering follows a strict yet flexible system which reduces the burden on both the students as well as the faculty incharge. The students are required to have a minimum of 85% attendance in every course.Students whose attendance is between 75% to 85% can apply for condonation. If the students fail to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance then the student will be in-eligible to take up the semester end examination.Under special circumstances if the student fails to maintain 75% attendance due to valid reasons then the student can contact their proctor to discuss the same.
